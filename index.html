About Us

Namma Bengaluru is a website that aims to provide a one-stop website for information about Bangalore. The website provides information about accommodations, tourist spots, and local events and happenings. It allows users to rent cars for a specified time period, and allows local businesses to post about offers and discounts.

The main goal of our website is to consolidate all relevant information about Bangalore that tourists or locals would need into one spot. This would allow tourists to feel comfortable in a new city quicker, and benefit locals by keeping them informed about events happening in their city.
